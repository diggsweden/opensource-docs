Pointers to other Swedish public sector organisations and their collaboration spaces on code platforms (GitHub, GitLab, etc.).
The list is **not exhaustive and not verified** — use it as a starting point for finding what others share openly. Organisation names, links and activity status may have changed since the source was published.

A **V** after a platform link marks that the organisation had visible activity in the previous two years according to the source.

Source: extracted from Annex I of the report [*Kodsamverkansplattform för öppen programvara — 1.0.0*](https://github.com/diggsweden/ena-samverkansplattform-os/blob/main/Kodsamverkansplattform_1.0.md), Ena working group, 2025 *(the working repository is now archived)*.
Missing your organisation? Please open an issue at [`diggsweden/opensource-docs`](https://github.com/diggsweden/opensource-docs/issues).
